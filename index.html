TEAM ALPHA is a dedicated group from the Department of Computer Science, Sidho-Kanho-Birsha University, committed to raising awareness about Artificial Intelligence among local communities.

Our Mission

To educate and empower people with fundamental knowledge of AI, making technology accessible for all.

Our Vision

To create AI-literate communities and inspire future innovators from rural and urban areas alike.

Our Team

A passionate team of students and researchers from SKBU, dedicated to spreading AI awareness and conducting impactful outreach programs.
